Simply Notes

Xi Simply Notes is an unusual application that lets you create text, image, and map notes. Copy, share, or delete the notes easily with one swipe.

You can swipe right to copy, swipe right further to share, and swipe left to delete the notes. Your notes are private and inaccessible by other applications unless you share them.

You can select an image or take a photograph to save as an image note.

When you create a map note, the application is going to drop a marker to your current location. You can tap and hold anywhere on the map to change the marker location before you save the map note.

Map Links Analyzer

This application has a built-in map links analyzer that enables you to save different types of links as map notes. This way, when you share content including location, Xi Simply Notes is going to pinpoint that location on a map.

Interaction with Google Maps

Tap on the dropped marker in the middle of the map to see the Google Map interaction buttons. The buttons let you see the map location in the Google Maps application and show you directions to reach the location.
